Higher, Lower means to take your CS:GO expertise to play and guess which CS:GO2 item is worth more than the other. We have leaderboards displaying every users score, as well as other games to play such as memory, wordle and a quiz, suitable for all kinds of Counter Strike 2 players. By using csfloat.com's API, we fetch the item's currency. We took blast.tv's conecpt as inspiration and made out own twist on it.

Higher, Lower is a simple game where you get two items and you have to guess which one has the highest value. As one continues, you can gain streaks. Compete with other players to reach the top of the learderboard.

The only data stored are user scores to keep the learderboard up-to-date. We do not require or share any other data.

We decided to make this website as a fun game for CS:GO players where they can interact with others and also compete. It's a place to hang out with liked-minded individuals and play fun games.
